About

Deidre Shauna Lynch is Ernest Bernbaum Professor of English Literature at Harvard University. In 2021 she was also appointed as a Harvard College Professor, in recognition of her excellence in undergraduate teaching. She has published widely on the history and theory of the novel, on womenâ€™s writing, on the history of books and reading. Her books include and Loving Literature: A Cultural History and The Economy of Character: Novels, Market Culture, and the Business of Inner Meaning, as well as multiple editions and essay collections, including, most recently, Mansfield Park: An Annotated Edition and The Unfinished Book (co-edited with Alexandra Gillespie). She is the recipient of fellowships from the National Humanities Center and the John Simon Guggenheim Memorial Foundation and has won multiple teaching awards.
